Hudson Stingers Robotics Blog

Follow FRC Team 4295 through kickoff, brainstorming, building, testing, and competitions.

October 1, 2023 – Kickoff Event

We attended the FIRST Robotics Competition Kickoff and received the details for this year’s challenge: Crescendo! Our team is buzzing with ideas already. Stay tuned for updates on our progress.

October 10, 2023 – Brainstorming Session

We held our first major brainstorming session today. After lively discussion, we’ve developed a basic design for our robot. Next steps: creating detailed plans and starting the build.

October 20, 2023 – Building Begins

Today we started building our robot for the Crescendo challenge. It’s a lot of hard work, but our team spirit is high and we’re ready for the challenge.

November 5, 2023 – Halfway Point

We’re halfway through build season! The frame is complete, and we’ve started working on electronics and programming. Progress is strong, and we’re learning every day.

November 20, 2023 – Testing Phase

The robot is built, and we’ve moved on to testing. A few hiccups, but nothing we can’t fix. Each challenge makes us stronger.

December 10, 2023 – Competition Day

Competition day! Our robot performed amazingly against tough competition. The whole team is proud of what we’ve achieved this season.
