October 1, 2023 - Kickoff Event

We attended the FRC Kickoff event today and received the details for this year’s challenge: Crescendo! It’s an exciting theme with lots of possibilities for creativity and innovation. Our team is buzzing with ideas already. Stay tuned for updates on our progress!

October 10, 2023 - Brainstorming Session

We held our first major brainstorming session today. After a lot of lively discussion, we’ve come up with a basic design for our robot. Next steps: creating detailed plans and starting the build. We’re all super excited to see how our ideas translate into the real world.

October 20, 2023 - Building Begins

Today we started building our robot for the Crescendo challenge. It’s going to be a lot of hard work, but everyone is diving in with enthusiasm. Our team spirit is high, and we’re ready to tackle any challenges that come our way.

November 5, 2023 - Halfway Point

We’re halfway through our build season, and things are looking good. The frame is complete, and we’ve started working on the electronics and programming. There’s still a lot to do, but we’re making great progress!

November 20, 2023 - Testing Phase

The robot is built, and we’ve moved on to the testing phase. This is where we find out if our design holds up under competition conditions. There have been a few hiccups, but nothing we can’t fix. We’re learning a lot and getting better every day.

December 10, 2023 - Competition Day

Today was competition day, and our robot performed amazingly! We encountered some tough competition but held our own. The whole team is proud of what we’ve achieved. Here’s to another successful season!
