Team Blog

Follow Hudson Stingers through kickoff, brainstorming, building, testing, and competitions.

October 1, 2023

Kickoff Event

We attended the FIRST Robotics Competition Kickoff and received the details for this year's challenge: Crescendo! Our team is buzzing with ideas already. The energy was electric as we brainstormed initial concepts and discussed strategy. Stay tuned for updates on our progress.

October 10, 2023

Brainstorming Session

We held our first major brainstorming session today. After lively discussion and debate, we've developed a basic design for our robot. The team showed incredible creativity and critical thinking. Next steps: creating detailed CAD models and starting procurement for components.

October 20, 2023

Building Begins

Today we started building our robot for the Crescendo challenge. It's a lot of hard work, but our team spirit is high and we're ready for the challenge ahead. The machine shop is buzzing with activity as students learn welding, machining, and assembly techniques from our mentors. Progress is steady!

November 5, 2023

Halfway Point

We're halfway through build season! The frame is complete, and we've started working on electronics and programming. Our electrical team has implemented the main control systems, while our programmers are developing autonomous routines. Progress is strong, and we're learning every day.

November 20, 2023

Testing Phase

The robot is built, and we've moved on to testing. We've discovered a few issues that need addressing, but nothing we can't fix with the time we have left. Each challenge makes us stronger as a team, and our students are becoming more confident in troubleshooting and problem-solving.

December 10, 2023

Competition Day

Competition day! Our robot performed amazingly against tough competition. The whole team is proud of what we've achieved this season. From the first kickoff to the final match, it's been an incredible journey of learning, growth, and teamwork. Already looking forward to next year!
