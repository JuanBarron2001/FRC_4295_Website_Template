Hudson Stingers Robotics

Hudson High School’s official FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) Team 4295 in Hudson, Texas.

Welcome to Our Team

Hudson Stingers Robotics is powered by passionate students and dedicated mentors, working together to build competitive robots and inspire STEM education in our community.

Mission and Vision

Mission Statement

At Hudson Stingers Robotics, we’re buzzing with a passion for science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). Through hands-on learning, teamwork, and mentorship, we prepare students to innovate, problem-solve, and compete at the highest levels of FRC.

Vision Statement

We envision a future where every student has the opportunity to explore STEM fields, supported by mentors and peers. Our goal is to create a lasting legacy of innovation, teamwork, and excellence that extends beyond competitions into the wider Hudson community.
