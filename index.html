Mission & Vision

Mission

At Hudson Stingers Robotics, we're buzzing with a passion for science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). Through hands-on learning, teamwork, and mentorship, we prepare students to innovate, problem-solve, and compete at the highest levels of FRC.

Vision

We envision a future where every student has the opportunity to explore STEM fields, supported by mentors and peers. Our goal is to create a lasting legacy of innovation, teamwork, and excellence that extends beyond competitions.
