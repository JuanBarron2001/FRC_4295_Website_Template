About Hudson Stingers

Founded in 2012, we've been inspiring students and pushing innovation limits for over a decade.

The Robotics Lab

Science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields are among the fastest-growing industries, yet many students hesitate to pursue them. Hudson Stingers Robotics is determined to change that by sparking enthusiasm and providing hands-on opportunities for every interested student in our community.

Our History

Founded in 2012, Hudson Stingers Robotics (FRC Team 4295) has proudly represented Hudson High School for over a decade. Each year, we take on the challenge of the FIRST Robotics Competition, where students design, build, and program a robot in just six weeks to compete in alliance-based games. Our journey has been one of continuous growth and improvement.

Growth & Achievements

Since our founding, we've grown in both student members and mentors, improving our global competition rankings each year. Our success comes from intensive hands-on learning in engineering, programming, CAD, project management, and business skills. Beyond technical expertise, our students gain life skills such as ingenuity, responsibility, and resourcefulness that prepare them for any challenge.

What Makes Us Different

Innovation First

We prioritize creative problem-solving and cutting-edge engineering principles in everything we build.

Team Spirit

Our success is built on collaboration, mentorship, and supporting each other to reach our collective goals.

Future Ready

We prepare students not just for competitions, but for careers in technology and engineering fields.
