What is FIRST Robotics?

The ultimate Sport for the Mind

The Challenge

Combining the excitement of sport with the rigor of science and technology, FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) challenges high school students to design, build, and program industrial-size robots to compete in a new game each year. Students call it "the hardest fun you'll ever have."

Engineering

Under strict rules, limited time, and limited resources, teams design and build competitive robots that solve real-world challenges presented in the annual game.

Mentorship

Volunteer professional mentors guide students, making FRC as close to real-world engineering as a student can get outside of university or industry.

Business Skills

Teams raise funds, design team brands, and develop business plansâ€”skills essential for any technical career or startup.

Competition

Each season culminates in the thrilling FIRST Championship where the best teams from around the world compete at the highest level.
